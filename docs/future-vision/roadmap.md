# MQI Future-State Roadmap

## Purpose
Preserve the long-term roadmap implied by the current prototype and deck.

## Roadmap arc
### Phase 1 — Quoting workflow intelligence
- ingest RFQ and source materials
- normalize requirements
- evaluate feasible manufacturing paths
- produce approval-ready recommendations
- generate customer-facing quote output

### Phase 2 — Multi-manufacturer recommendation
- compare multiple qualified manufacturers in one workspace
- score capacity, inventory, lead time, quality, and commercial risk across candidates
- produce transparent best-path recommendations

### Phase 3 — Manufacturing network intelligence
- expand into sourcing intelligence
- expand into pricing intelligence
- expand into supplier performance visibility
- support predictive recommendations across a broader manufacturing network

### Phase 4 — Distributed fulfillment optimization
- support routing and distribution decisions
- optimize work allocation across a broader fulfillment/manufacturing network
- move from quote recommendation to fulfillment-aware network optimization

## Preservation note
This roadmap is the strategic future-state path and should remain intact even if the production MVP is narrowed to a single-manufacturer operating model.
