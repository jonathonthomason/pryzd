## Purpose
Preserve the current strategic/stakeholder deck before any MVP narrative realignment.

## Deck framing preserved
The current deck frames MQI as an AI-first product design case study and manufacturing decision platform that protects margin by improving quoting workflow speed, trust, and decision quality.

## Slide narrative archive
### Slide 1 — Margin stabilization
MQI is positioned around margin stabilization as the north-star KPI.

### Slide 2 — Current manual quoting process
The deck establishes current-state pain: sales, sourcing, and operations manually reconstruct quotes across fragmented data sources.

### Slide 3 — Volatility and decision pressure
The core problem is framed as human decision-making under uncertainty, not just quote generation.

### Slide 4 — Workflow + specialized agents
The operator-facing workflow is presented alongside a behind-the-scenes agent chain. This includes explicit manufacturer assessment and agent orchestration concepts.

### Slide 5 — Human judgment + system evaluation
The trust model is preserved: the system contributes recommendations, confidence, rationale, and approval support; humans retain accountability.

### Slide 6 — Design system foundations
The deck preserves token-based product system thinking intended to support both near-term prototyping and future platform scale.

### Slide 7 — Two clear outcomes
The workflow is shown delivering both customer-facing speed and internal decision confidence.

### Slide 8 — Architecture in support of workflow
The deck explicitly preserves orchestration, agent, platform, and roadmap layers.

### Slide 9 — Platform expansion vision
The deck preserves future-state expansion into:
- manufacturer matching
- sourcing intelligence
- pricing intelligence
- supplier performance insight
- predictive recommendations

### Slide 10 — Advanced architecture diagram
A detailed architecture diagram is included as the technical reference backing the broader platform story.

## Strategic positioning preserved
The deck currently positions MQI as:
- a workflow-first quoting experience
- an AI-supported decision product
- a platform-ready manufacturing intelligence system
- a wedge into broader sourcing and manufacturer intelligence
